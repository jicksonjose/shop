Responsible Tourism Spice Shop is also known as Kudumbashree. Kudumbashree is the poverty eradication and women empowerment programme implemented by the State Poverty Eradication Mission (SPEM) of the Government of Kerala. The name Kudumbashree in Malayalam language means 'prosperity of the family'.

The journey of RESPONSIBLE TOURISM SPICE SHOP starts back at 15 years ago. Our shop is managed by small team of passionate women entrepreneurs. Trip advisors authentically suggest [Trip-Adviser]. We source our products directly from farmers so that you can experience the benefits of eating healthy. Spices in here run the gamut from the familiar Nutmeg & Kashmir Saffron to the less common Garam Masala and Turmeric which are great in preparing flavourful and intense dishes. We provide exceptional products at a fair price all over India.
We also offer other products which have been added to our portfolio based on interest from our loyal customers such as Homemade Chocolates , Coffee,Dry fruits,Flavored Tea. We also have different kinds of Hair oil,Massage oil, Pain relief oil,Eucalyptus oil with a blissful blend of Ayurveda & also variety of handmade bathing products. We look forward to the support of customers like you to help us build this sustainable ecosystem.
